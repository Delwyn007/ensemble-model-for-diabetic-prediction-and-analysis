Project Description


Diabetes is a chronic disease and a major health issue among this hustle and stressful world.
So we have come up with a solution, where diabetes can be predicted with an ensemble model, with high accuracy.
We use KNN(k nearest neighbor) & LightGBM algorithms to predict whether the individual is sufffering from Diabetes or not.
